Privacy Policy for Prime Todo

Last updated: October 21, 2025

Introduction

Premium Designers ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Prime Todo mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Prime Todo does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Storage

  • All data created in Prime Todo (todos, categories, due dates, priorities) is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No user accounts or authentication required

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Advertising data
  • Any user data whatsoever

Offline Functionality

Prime Todo is a completely offline application:

  • Works without internet connection
  • No network permissions required
  • All data remains on your device
  • Data is never synchronized to cloud services

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data security depends on your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device lock screen protection
  • Data is stored using standard Android SQLite database
  • Data can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, our App is safe for use by children.

Third-Party Services

Prime Todo does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline without any external dependencies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps > Prime Todo
  3. Tap "Clear Data" or "Storage" > "Clear All Data"
  4. Or simply uninstall the app

All your data will be permanently deleted from your device.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data:

  • There is no data to access
  • There is no data to correct
  • There is no data to delete from our servers (because we don't have servers)
  • There is no data to export
